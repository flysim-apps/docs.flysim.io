# Calendar & Flight Scheduling
{: .no_toc }

## Table of contents
{: .no_toc .text-delta }

1. TOC
{:toc}

---

The **Calendar** lets you plan and schedule flights in advance — privately or as coordinated group events with other pilots.

{: .important }
The Calendar feature requires an active FlyAround subscription. Free trial accounts may have restricted access.

---

## Opening the Calendar

Click **Calendar** in the main navigation menu.

---

## Calendar Views

### Month View
- Shows the current month with all scheduled events on their dates
- Click any date to see that day's events or create a new one

### Week View Panel
- A 7-day timeline alongside the month view
- Hour-by-hour scheduling grid
- Color-coded blocks for busy/available slots
- Click an empty slot to create an event at that time

### Upcoming Events Sidebar
- Lists your next scheduled events in chronological order
- Shows event title, date/time, and type

---

## Creating a Flight Event

1. Click an empty slot in the Calendar or use the **Create Event** button
2. Fill in the event details:
   - **Title / Flight Description**
   - **Start date and time**
   - **End date and time**
   - **Event type** (private or group/shared)
3. Optionally attach an OFP to the event
4. Save the event

---

## Group Flights

With **Group Flights** enabled, you can:
- Create **shared events** that other pilots can join
- View the attendee list for a group flight event
- Invite other pilots to join your scheduled flight

---

## Pending Invitations

Open **Pending Events** from the Calendar section to manage invitations:
- View the event details (briefing, route, time)
- **Accept** to join the group flight
- **Reject** to decline

---

## Event Details View

Click any calendar event to open the Event Details dialog:
- Full event summary and briefing
- Date and time *(displayed in UTC)*
- Attendee list (for group flights)
- Associated OFP document
- Edit or delete options

---

## Starting a Scheduled Flight

When a scheduled event is approaching:
1. Load the correct aircraft in MSFS
2. Open the **Dispatcher Wizard** ("Time to Fly" on the map)
3. The associated route will be pre-filled from the calendar event
4. Complete the Dispatcher steps and confirm

After completing the flight, it links to the calendar event in your Logbook — visible under the **Schedule** tab of the flight detail view.

---

## Calendar Notifications

FlyAround sends in-app reminders before scheduled events so you have time to load your aircraft and prepare.

---

## Tips

- All times are displayed in **UTC** for consistency across time zones.
- Group flights are great for coordinating with friends or virtual airline crews.
- You can have multiple events on the same day but only one active flight at a time.
