Manage your pilot profile, personal information, subscription status, and billing details from the **Profile** settings page.

---

## Opening Your Profile

Click the **edit icon** next to your name in the main navigation menu header.

---

## Profile Tab

### Editable Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Nickname** | Your display name shown to other pilots and on the map |
| **Handle** | Your unique pilot handle / callsign *(set once — cannot be changed after initial setup)* |
| **About** | A short bio visible on your public profile |
| **Avatar** | Upload or update your profile picture |

Click **Save** to apply changes.

### Pilot Statistics *(read-only)*

Updated automatically after each completed flight:
- **Total Flights** — Number of completed flights
- **Flight Hours** — Total Hobbs time
- **Distance Traveled** — Total nautical miles flown

---

## Billing Tab

*(Visible only if the Billing feature is enabled on your account)*

### Account Status

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Active** | Full access to all subscription features |
| **Trial** | Free trial period — expiration date shown |
| **Limited** | Trial expired or billing inactive — features restricted |

{: .warning }
When your account is **Limited**, a banner appears across the app and features such as Calendar and flight dispatch become unavailable.

### Billing Information

- **Legal Name** — Registered name on the billing account
- **Billing Address** — Address associated with your payment method

### Subscription Management

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Current Plan** | View your active subscription details |
| **Billing Offers** | Browse upgrade options or add-on features |
| **Activate Billing Code** | Enter a promo or redemption code |
| **Invoice History** | View and download past invoices |
| **Cancel Subscription** | Cancel your current plan *(with confirmation dialog)* |

---

## Tips

- **Handle**: Choose carefully — it cannot be changed after it is set.
- **Billing codes**: Enter promo or voucher codes in the "Activate Billing Code" field under the Billing tab.
- **Trial expiration**: Check the expiration date shown in the Billing tab to avoid unexpected feature lockouts.
- **Cancellation**: After cancelling, access continues until the end of the current billing period. Your Logbook and Hangar data are always preserved.
