# Live Map
{: .no_toc }

## Table of contents
{: .no_toc .text-delta }

1. TOC
{:toc}

---

The **Live Map** is the home screen of FlyAround. It shows real-time global flight activity and lets you track your own flight, explore other pilots' flights, and view airport information.

---

## Accessing the Live Map

The Live Map is the **default screen** when you open FlyAround. Return to it any time by selecting **Live Map** from the main navigation menu.

---

## What You See on the Map

- **Flight position markers** — Aircraft icons showing active flights from all connected pilots
- **Route lines** — Planned route paths
- **Airport markers** — Selectable airport icons
- **Your aircraft** — Highlighted separately when you have an active flight

---

## Search

The **Search Box** at the top of the map lets you:
- Search for a specific flight by callsign or flight ID
- Search for airports by ICAO/IATA code or name
- Jump to any location on the map

---

## Interacting with Flights

Click any flight marker to see a **Flight Info Popover**:
- Callsign
- Pilot name
- Route (origin → destination)
- Aircraft type
- Current altitude and speed
- Flight phase

Click through to open the full **Flight Detail View**.

---

## Interacting with Airports

Click any airport marker to view:
- Airport name and ICAO code
- Active routes from/to the airport
- Historical flight statistics
- A route map showing all paths in and out

---

## Pilot Summary View

Click on another pilot's flight to open their **Pilot Summary**:
- Pilot nickname and avatar
- Total flights, flight hours, distance traveled
- Follower/following counts
- Last activity timestamp

---

## Your Active Flight Controls

When you have an active flight, the **Speed Dial** (floating button menu) provides map-specific controls:

| Button | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **3D Map** | Toggle between 2D and 3D map view |
| **My Location** | Re-center the map on your aircraft |
| **LiveMap** | Open the full live tracking map |
| **Share** | Copy a public link to your flight |

See [Active Flight]({% link docs/active-flight.md %}) for the complete Speed Dial reference.

---

## Sharing Your Flight

During an active flight, use **Share** in the Speed Dial to copy a link to your live flight. Anyone with the link can watch your flight on the public FlyAround map at `flyon.me`.

---

## Tips

- The Live Map updates in real time — no need to refresh.
- All FlyAround pilots worldwide are visible on the map.
- Use **My Location** to re-center after panning away from your aircraft.
