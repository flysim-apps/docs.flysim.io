# Billing & Account FAQ
{: .no_toc }

1. TOC
{:toc}

---

## Is FlyAround free?

FlyAround offers a **free trial** with access to most features for a limited period. After the trial, a subscription is required for premium features. Check your current status in **Profile → Billing**.

---

## What features require a subscription?

- Flight Calendar & Scheduling
- Group Flights
- AI Assistant
- Unrestricted flight dispatch

---

## What happens when my trial expires?

Your account becomes **Limited**:
- A "Account is Limited" banner appears at the top of the app
- New flight dispatch may be blocked
- Calendar and scheduling become unavailable
- Your Logbook and Hangar data remain intact

Go to **Profile → Billing** and activate a plan to restore full access.

---

## How do I check my account status?

1. Click the edit icon next to your name in the navigation menu
2. Open the **Billing** tab
3. Status is shown as: **Active**, **Trial** (with expiration date), or **Limited**

---

## How do I activate a subscription?

1. Open **Profile → Billing** tab
2. Browse **Billing Offers**
3. Select a plan and follow the payment prompts
4. Account status updates to **Active** immediately after successful payment

---

## I have a promo or billing code — how do I use it?

1. Open **Profile → Billing** tab
2. Find **"Activate Billing Code"**
3. Enter your code and confirm

The associated plan or extension is applied to your account.

---

## How do I view my invoice history?

Open **Profile → Billing** tab → **Invoice History**. Click any invoice to view or download it.

---

## How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Open **Profile → Billing** tab
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm in the dialog

Access continues until the end of the current billing period.

---

## Will I lose my data if I cancel?

No. Your Logbook, Hangar, and pilot profile are preserved regardless of billing status. Cancellation only restricts access to premium features.

---

## My account shows "Limited" but I have an active subscription

Try:
1. Log out and log back in
2. Wait a few minutes for the billing system to sync
3. If the issue persists, contact FlyAround support with your account email and subscription confirmation
