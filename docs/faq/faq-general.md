## What is FlyAround?

FlyAround is a flight tracking and management platform for Microsoft Flight Simulator (MSFS) 2020/2024, X-Plane 11/12, and Prepar3D. It runs as a Windows desktop app alongside your simulator and adds a full operations layer: flight planning, cargo/passenger loading, real-time position tracking, flight logging, and community features like group flights and live maps.

---

## Who is FlyAround for?

FlyAround is designed for flight simulation enthusiasts who want more structure and realism. It suits:
- Solo pilots who want to log and track their flights
- Virtual airline members who need scheduling and OFP tools
- Streamers who want a live HUD overlay for their audience
- Community pilots who fly in coordinated groups

---

## Does FlyAround work with any aircraft add-on?

Yes. FlyAround works with any aircraft loaded in MSFS. You register your aircraft in the Hangar using the airframe type, airline livery, and tail number — it does not need to match a specific add-on package.

---

## Do I need to be online to use FlyAround?

Yes. FlyAround requires an active internet connection for authentication, flight data sync, OFP generation, and the live map. The simulator itself can run offline, but FlyAround's tracking and logging features require connectivity.

---

## What simulator is supported?

FlyAround currently supports **Microsoft Flight Simulator (MSFS)**. The app communicates with the simulator in real time via a local backend service.

---

## Is FlyAround free?

FlyAround offers a **free trial** that gives access to most features. After the trial, a subscription is required for:
- Flight Calendar and scheduling
- Group Flights
- AI Assistant

Visit the **Billing** tab in your Profile settings to view available plans.

## How do I get support?

- **Discord** — Join the FlyAround community Discord (link in the app under Support)
- **Email** — Contact the support team directly (email listed in the app under Support)

---

## How do I update FlyAround?

FlyAround checks for new versions automatically on launch. If an update is available, a notification appears with release notes and a download link.

---

## Can I use FlyAround while streaming?

Yes. Configure the **streaming overlay** under **Settings → Overlay** and capture the overlay URL as a browser source in OBS or Streamlabs. The overlay displays your live flight data for viewers.
