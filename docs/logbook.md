# Logbook

## Table of contents
{: .no_toc .text-delta }

1. TOC
{:toc}

---

The **Logbook** is a complete record of all your completed flights. Every flight you finish through FlyAround is automatically logged here with detailed statistics.

---

## Opening the Logbook

Click **Logbook** in the main navigation menu.

---

## Logbook Table

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Callsign** | Flight number — click to open full flight details |
| **Route** | Origin → Destination with flight rating (0–5 stars) |
| **Date** | Date and time the flight was completed |
| **Duration** | Total flight time in HH:MM format |
| **Distance** | Distance traveled in nautical miles (nm) |
| **Used Fuel** | Fuel consumed during the flight |
| **Landing Rate** | Landing quality shown as a colored chip |
| **Delete** | Remove the flight entry from your logbook |

---

## Landing Rate Color Codes

| Color | Quality |
|-------|---------|
| Green | Smooth landing |
| Yellow | Acceptable landing |
| Red | Hard landing |

---

## Flight Detail View

Click any **Callsign** to open the full flight details page:

| Tab | Contents |
|-----|---------|
| **Overview** | Aircraft, flight number, tail number, cost index, cruise altitude, distance |
| **Timeline** | Visual timeline of all flight phases |
| **Loadsheet** | Weight & balance, fuel burn, payload breakdown |
| **OFP** | Full Operational Flight Plan document |
| **PAX** | Passenger manifest *(PAX flights only)* |
| **Weather** | METAR at departure and arrival airports |
| **Tracking** | Map showing the actual flight path flown |
| **Schedule** | Related calendar event *(if flight was part of a scheduled event)* |
| **Airports** | Departure and arrival airport details |
| **Report Summary** | Final flight statistics and performance summary |
| **Data Block** | Raw real-time flight parameter data |

---

## Flight Rating

Each flight receives a rating from **0 to 5 stars** (0.1 precision) shown next to the route. The rating reflects overall flight performance including landing smoothness, flight plan adherence, and other tracked factors.

---

## Deleting a Flight Record

Click the **Delete** icon in the logbook row and confirm in the dialog.

{: .warning }
Deletion from the Logbook is **permanent** and cannot be undone.

---

## Pilot Statistics

Your cumulative stats are shown in your **pilot profile** and update after every completed flight:
- Total number of completed flights
- Total flight hours (Hobbs time)
- Total distance traveled (nm)
